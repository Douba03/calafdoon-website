Delete Your Account

We're sorry to see you go. Here's how to permanently delete your Calafdoon account.

⚠️ Important: Account Deletion is Permanent

Once you delete your account, this action cannot be undone. All your data will be permanently removed, including:

  • Your profile information and photos
  • All messages and conversations
  • Connection requests (sent and received)
  • Match history
  • Premium subscription (no refunds)

Before You Delete

Consider these alternatives:

  • Pause Your Account: Hide your profile temporarily without losing your data (Settings > Account > Pause Account)
  • Adjust Privacy Settings: Control who can see your profile and contact you
  • Contact Support: If you're experiencing issues, we're here to help at sheeko66931@hotmail.com

How to Delete Your Account

1

Open the Calafdoon App

Launch the app on your device and log in to your account.

2

Go to Settings

Tap your profile icon in the bottom navigation, then tap the settings gear icon (⚙️).

3

Navigate to Account Settings

Scroll down and tap "Account" or "Account Settings."

4

Select Delete Account

Scroll to the bottom and tap "Delete Account."

5

Confirm Deletion

Read the warning message carefully. If you're sure, tap "Delete My Account" and confirm your password.

6

Account Deleted

Your account and all associated data will be permanently deleted within 30 days. You'll receive a confirmation email.

What Happens After Deletion?

  • Immediate: Your profile is hidden from all users and you're logged out
  • Within 30 days: All your data is permanently deleted from our servers
  • Subscriptions: Active Premium subscriptions are cancelled (no refunds issued)
  • Messages: Your messages will be removed from other users' conversations
  • Re-registration: You can create a new account anytime, but previous data cannot be recovered

Data Retention

We may retain certain information as required by law or for legitimate business purposes, such as:

  • Transaction records (for accounting and tax purposes)
  • Reports of abuse or violations (for safety and legal compliance)
  • Anonymized analytics data (no personal identifiers)

For more details, see our Privacy Policy.

Cancel Premium Subscription

If you have an active Premium subscription, you must cancel it separately through the App Store:

  1. Open iPhone Settings
  2. Tap your name at the top
  3. Tap "Subscriptions"
  4. Select "Calafdoon"
  5. Tap "Cancel Subscription"

Note: Deleting your account does not automatically cancel your subscription or issue refunds.
