Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Calafdoon

About Calafdoon

What is Calafdoon?

Calafdoon is the premier Muslim dating and marriage app designed for singles seeking meaningful, faith-centered relationships. We provide a respectful, halal environment to connect with compatible matches who share your values and beliefs.

Who can use Calafdoon?

Calafdoon is for Muslim singles aged 18+ who are looking for marriage or serious relationships. Whether you're seeking marriage, looking for a serious relationship, or a diaspora Muslim connecting with your culture, Calafdoon is designed for you.

Is Calafdoon free to use?

Yes! Calafdoon offers a free version where you can create a profile, browse matches, and send up to 10 connection requests per day. We also offer Premium membership with unlimited requests, advanced filters, profile views, priority visibility, and read receipts.

Getting Started

How do I create an account?

Download the Calafdoon app from the App Store, tap "Sign Up," and provide your email, phone number, and basic information. You'll receive a verification code to confirm your account. Then complete your profile with photos and details about your faith, values, and preferences.

I didn't receive my verification code. What should I do?

Check your spam/junk folder. Ensure you entered the correct phone number or email. Wait a few minutes and request a new code. If the issue persists, contact us at sheeko66931@hotmail.com.

What information do I need to provide?

You'll share your religious practice level, prayer frequency, hijab/modesty preferences, cultural background, education, family goals, marriage timeline, interests, and lifestyle preferences. The more complete your profile, the better your matches!

Can I use Calafdoon without uploading photos?

While you can create a profile without photos, we strongly recommend adding at least one photo. Profiles with photos receive significantly more connection requests and responses.

Matching & Connections

How does matching work?

Our smart matching algorithm considers religious compatibility (prayer frequency, hijab preferences, halal lifestyle), cultural background, location preferences, age range, marriage timeline, education level, and lifestyle choices to suggest compatible profiles.

What is a connection request?

Instead of swiping, you send thoughtful connection requests with a personalized message. This encourages quality interactions. Free users can send 10 requests per day; Premium members get unlimited requests.

How do I send a connection request?

Browse profiles, tap the diamond "Request" button, write a personalized message explaining why you're interested, and send. The other person can accept, decline, or ignore your request.

What happens when someone accepts my request?

You'll receive a notification and can start messaging! You can send text messages, voice messages, and share photos in a private, secure chat.

Can I filter matches?

Yes! Use filters for religious practice level, prayer frequency, hijab preferences, cultural background, age range, location/distance, education level, and marriage timeline. Premium members get access to advanced filters.

How many profiles can I view per day?

Free users have unlimited profile browsing. Premium members also get to see who viewed their profile.

Privacy & Safety

Is my information safe?

Yes. We use industry-standard encryption, never share your data with third parties without consent, and allow you to control profile visibility. Read our full Privacy Policy for details.

Can I control who sees my profile?

Yes! You can hide your profile from specific users, control distance visibility, and choose whether to show your last active status.

How do I block or report someone?

Go to their profile, tap the three dots (⋯) menu, and select "Block" or "Report." Blocked users cannot see your profile or contact you. Reported users are reviewed by our moderation team.

What should I report?

Report harassment or inappropriate messages, fake profiles or scams, disrespectful behavior, requests for money, or anything that violates our Community Guidelines.

Does Calafdoon verify profiles?

We offer optional profile verification. Verified users get a checkmark badge. We review photos and may request additional verification steps to ensure authenticity.

Messaging & Communication

How do I start a conversation?

You can only message someone after they accept your connection request (or you accept theirs). This ensures both parties are interested before chatting.

Can I send photos in messages?

Yes, once connected, you can share photos, voice messages, and text messages in your private chat.

Can I video call on Calafdoon?

Video calling is not currently available but may be added in future updates. For now, you can exchange contact information with matches if you both feel comfortable.

What are read receipts?

Read receipts show when someone has read your message. This feature is available for Premium members.

Premium Membership

What is Calafdoon Premium?

Premium is our subscription plan offering unlimited connection requests, see who viewed your profile, advanced filters, priority visibility in search results, read receipts, and no ads.

How much does Premium cost?

Pricing is displayed in the app under Settings > Premium. We offer monthly, 3-month, and annual plans with discounts for longer commitments.

How do I subscribe to Premium?

Go to your profile, tap Settings > Upgrade to Premium, choose your plan, and complete payment through the App Store.

Can I cancel my Premium subscription?

Yes, anytime. Go to your iPhone Settings > [Your Name] > Subscriptions > Calafdoon > Cancel Subscription. You'll retain Premium benefits until the end of your billing period.

Do you offer refunds?

Refunds are handled by Apple through the App Store. Contact Apple Support for refund requests. We cannot process refunds directly.

Account Management

How do I edit my profile?

Tap your profile icon, then tap "Edit Profile." You can update photos, bio, religious preferences, and all other profile details.

How do I change my password?

Go to Settings > Account > Change Password. Enter your current password and new password, then save.

I forgot my password. What do I do?

On the login screen, tap "Forgot Password?" Enter your email or phone number, and we'll send you a reset link.

Can I pause my account?

Yes! Go to Settings > Account > Pause Account. Your profile will be hidden from search, but your data remains saved. You can unpause anytime.

How do I delete my account?

Visit our Account Deletion page for detailed instructions. You can delete your account in Settings > Account > Delete Account. This action is permanent and cannot be undone.

Technical Issues

The app isn't loading. What should I do?

Check your internet connection, close and restart the app, update to the latest version from the App Store, or restart your device. If issues persist, contact support.

I'm not receiving notifications.

Go to iPhone Settings > Notifications > Calafdoon and ensure notifications are enabled. Also check in-app Settings > Notifications to customize notification preferences.

My photos won't upload.

Ensure you've granted photo access: Settings > Calafdoon > Photos > Allow. Check that photos are under 10MB and in JPG/PNG format. Try a different photo or restart the app.

Location services aren't working.

Go to iPhone Settings > Privacy > Location Services > Calafdoon and select "While Using the App." Location helps us show you nearby matches.
