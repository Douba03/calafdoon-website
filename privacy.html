Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 13, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Calafdoon. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and services.

By using Calafdoon, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use our services.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

When you create an account and use Calafdoon, you provide us with:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, phone number, date of birth, gender
  • Profile Information: Photos, bio, religious practice level, prayer frequency, hijab/modesty preferences, cultural background, education, occupation, interests, lifestyle preferences
  • Religious & Cultural Data: Prayer frequency, hijab preferences, halal lifestyle choices, marriage timeline, family involvement preferences
  • Preferences: Age range, distance preferences, cultural preferences, religious compatibility preferences
  • Communications: Messages, voice messages, photos shared in conversations, connection request messages
  • Payment Information: Processed securely through Apple's App Store (we do not store credit card details)
  • Support Communications: Any information you provide when contacting customer support

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

When you use our app, we automatically collect:

  • Location Data: With your permission, we collect precise location data to show you nearby matches and calculate distance. You can disable location services in your device settings, but this may limit app functionality.
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, unique device identifiers, mobile network information
  • Usage Data: How you interact with the app, features used, profiles viewed, connection requests sent/received, time spent on app
  • Log Data: IP address, access times, app crashes, performance data
  • Photos & Camera: With your permission, we access your photo library and camera to upload profile photos and share images in conversations

2.3 Information from Third Parties

  • Apple Sign-In: If you sign in with Apple, we receive your name and email (or private relay email)
  • Social Media: If you choose to link social media accounts, we may receive basic profile information

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide Services: Create and manage your account, display your profile to potential matches, facilitate connections and messaging
  • Matching Algorithm: Use your preferences, location, religious practice, and cultural background to suggest compatible matches
  • Location-Based Features: Show you nearby users, calculate distance to matches, provide location-based search filters
  • Communication: Enable messaging, voice messages, and photo sharing between matched users
  • Safety & Security: Verify accounts, detect and prevent fraud, enforce our Terms of Service, investigate reports of abuse
  • Improve Services: Analyze usage patterns, develop new features, improve matching algorithms
  • Customer Support: Respond to your inquiries and provide assistance
  • Marketing: Send you updates, promotions, and information about Premium features (you can opt out)
  • Legal Compliance: Comply with legal obligations and protect our rights

4. How We Share Your Information

4.1 With Other Users

  • Your profile information, photos, and preferences are visible to other users based on your privacy settings
  • Your approximate location (city/distance) is shown to potential matches
  • Messages and photos you send are shared with your matched connections

4.2 Service Providers

We share information with third-party service providers who help us operate our app:

  • Cloud storage providers (for photos and data)
  • Analytics services (to understand app usage)
  • Customer support tools
  • Payment processors (Apple App Store)

4.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law or to:

  • Comply with legal processes (subpoenas, court orders)
  • Protect our rights, property, or safety
  • Investigate fraud or security issues
  • Respond to emergencies involving danger of death or serious injury

4.4 Business Transfers

If Calafdoon is involved in a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred. We will notify you before your information becomes subject to a different privacy policy.

4.5 With Your Consent

We may share your information for other purposes with your explicit consent.

5. Your Privacy Controls

You have control over your information:

  • Profile Visibility: Control who can see your profile and photos
  • Location Settings: Enable or disable location services in your device settings
  • Block Users: Prevent specific users from seeing your profile or contacting you
  • Photo Access: Control app access to your photo library in device settings
  • Notifications: Manage notification preferences in app settings
  • Marketing Communications: Opt out of promotional emails
  • Account Deletion: Permanently delete your account and data (see Account Deletion)

6. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. When you delete your account:

  • Your profile is immediately hidden from all users
  • Your data is permanently deleted within 30 days
  • We may retain certain information as required by law or for legitimate business purposes (e.g., transaction records, abuse reports)
  • Anonymized analytics data may be retained indefinitely

7. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption of data in transit (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Encryption of sensitive data at rest
  • Secure authentication and password hashing
  • Regular security audits and monitoring
  • Access controls limiting employee access to personal data

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Location Data

Calafdoon uses location data to enhance your experience:

  • Why We Collect: To show you nearby matches, calculate distance, and provide location-based search
  • Permission Required: We request location permission when you first use the app
  • How It's Used: Your approximate location (city/distance) is shown to potential matches; precise coordinates are not shared
  • Control: You can disable location services in Settings > Privacy > Location Services > Calafdoon
  • Impact of Disabling: Disabling location limits matching to users without location filters and hides distance information

9. Photos & Camera Access

We request access to your photos and camera to:

  • Upload profile photos from your photo library
  • Take new photos with your camera for your profile
  • Share photos in conversations with matched users

You can manage photo access in Settings > Privacy > Photos > Calafdoon. Denying access prevents you from uploading or sharing photos.

10. Children's Privacy

Calafdoon is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 18. If we discover that a child under 18 has provided us with personal information, we will delete it immediately. 11. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your country of residence. These countries may have different data protection laws. By using Calafdoon, you consent to the transfer of your information to these countries.

12. Your Rights

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal information
  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and data
  • Portability: Request your data in a portable format
  • Objection: Object to certain processing of your data
  • Restriction: Request restriction of processing in certain circumstances

To exercise these rights, contact us at sheeko66931@hotmail.com.

  • Right to opt-out of the sale of personal information (we do not sell your information)
  • Right to non-discrimination for exercising your rights

To exercise these rights, email sheeko66931@hotmail.com with "California Privacy Request" in the subject line.

14. European Privacy Rights (GDPR)

If you are in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), including the rights listed in Section 12. Our legal basis for processing your data includes:

  • Consent: You consent to our processing when you create an account
  • Contract: Processing is necessary to provide our services
  • Legitimate Interests: We process data to improve our services and ensure safety
